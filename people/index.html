Our Team

We're Will and Abbey, the duo behind Lucent Studio.

We started this studio out of a desire to work independently in doing what we love. What began as two parallel creative paths has become a collaborative practice, where design and development work side by side from the very start.

Abbey is our designer and illustrator.

She cares about how things look just as much as how they work. With studies in User Experience and a love for visual detail, Abbey designs websites that are clear, considered, and easy to navigate—marrying structure and style.

She's also passionate about branding, and can help with everything from illustration and logo design to full visual refreshes—bringing cohesion and personality to how your brand shows up.

Will is our developer.

He's the kind of person who codes for fun, and it shows in his work. His builds are fast and adaptable, designed to handle real-world use from performance to structure.

With an eye on analytics and close collaboration with Abbey's user experience-informed designs, Will ensures your site ranks well in search results and loads quickly so it works just as well for your audience as it does for you.
