Embody Healthcare

A complete brand refresh and digital transformation for a trusted Melbourne osteopathy practice with over 20 years of heritage.

View Live Site →

The Client

Embody Osteopathy is a well-established healthcare practice in Melbourne that has been serving the community for over two decades. They specialise in osteopathic treatment, helping patients of all ages manage pain, improve mobility, and maintain overall wellbeing.

After 20+ years in Greensborough, Embody was relocating to a new clinic in Eltham. They saw this as the perfect opportunity to refresh their entire brand—updating their logo, website, and all practice materials in one coordinated move. With a loyal client base built on trust and expertise, they needed their digital presence to reflect the quality and professionalism they deliver in every consultation.

The Challenge

After 20+ years in business, Embody's brand and website were showing their age. While their reputation remained strong, their online presence didn't match the modern, professional experience clients received in the clinic.

Key Issues:

  • Outdated visual identity: The existing branding felt dated and didn't convey the warmth and professionalism of the practice
  • Aging website: The site was difficult to navigate, not mobile-friendly, and didn't reflect current healthcare standards
  • Confusing information architecture: Clients struggled to find treatment information, book appointments, or understand services
  • Limited accessibility: The site wasn't optimised for older clients or those with accessibility needs—a significant portion of their demographic
  • Inconsistent brand materials: Business cards and signage didn't align, creating a disjointed brand experience

The goal was clear: modernise the brand and digital presence while honouring their 20+ year legacy and maintaining trust with existing clients.

Our Approach

We took a holistic approach to refreshing Embody's brand, starting with strategy and research before moving into design and development.

Discovery & Strategy

We began by understanding Embody's patients, values, and goals. Through conversations with the team and reviewing patient feedback, we identified what made Embody unique: a calm, professional environment where clients feel genuinely cared for.

Brand Refresh

We updated the existing logo to feel more contemporary while retaining brand recognition. The refreshed mark is cleaner, more versatile, and works beautifully across digital and print.

We developed a new colour palette that balances professionalism with warmth—soft, calming tones that reflect the therapeutic nature of osteopathy while maintaining medical credibility.

Website Redesign

The website was rebuilt from the ground up with a focus on clarity, accessibility, and user experience.

  • Simplified navigation: We streamlined the structure so patients can find what they need in seconds—treatment information, practitioner bios, or booking details
  • Mobile-first design: With many patients booking on the go, the site is fully responsive and works flawlessly on any device
  • Accessibility considerations: Larger text options, high contrast, and clear calls-to-action make the site easy to use for all ages
  • Fast performance: Optimised images and clean code ensure the site loads quickly, reducing bounce rates
  • SEO foundation: Proper structure and metadata help the site rank well for local healthcare searches

Print Materials

We designed new business cards and signage that carry the refreshed brand consistently across all touchpoints, creating a cohesive experience from first contact to in-clinic visit.

Services: Brand Strategy, Logo Redesign, Colour Palette, Website Design & Development, Content Strategy, SEO, Business Cards, Signage

The Results

The refreshed brand and new website transformed how patients experience Embody, both online and in person.

Immediate Impact:

  • Modern, trustworthy presence: The updated brand feels professional and approachable, building confidence with new patients while reassuring existing ones
  • Improved user experience: Patients can now easily find information, understand treatments, and book appointments without friction
  • Better accessibility: The site works seamlessly for all ages and abilities, reflecting Embody's inclusive approach to healthcare
  • Cohesive brand touchpoints: From website to business cards to clinic signage, every interaction reinforces the same professional, caring brand
  • Positive client feedback: Existing patients commented on the fresh, modern look while appreciating the familiar, trusted service

Most importantly, the digital transformation positions Embody to continue serving the community for decades to come, with a modern platform that can grow alongside the business.
