How it Works

01

Planning

We start by getting to know your business, your goals, and your audience. Through open communication, we'll map out a plan that aligns with your vision and ensures a smooth journey from start to finish. This stage is all about building trust and clarity.

02

Designing

This is where your ideas start to take shape. We create designs tailored to your business, collaborating with you every step of the way. Your feedback is crucial here, and we'll make adjustments until you're 100% happy with the look and feel of your site.

03

Building

Once the design is finalised, we bring it to life. Every part of your website is carefully crafted to ensure it looks beautiful and functions perfectly, from fast loading times to effortless navigation. We handle all the technical details, so you don't have to worry.

04

Maintenance

After your website is live, we offer ongoing maintenance to keep it running smoothly. From updates to troubleshooting, we've got you covered for a small recurring fee. That way, you can focus on growing your business while we handle the rest.

We prioritise balancing aesthetics with performance

At Lucent Studio, we work with what you have—whether it's a clear vision or just an idea. Our goal is to refresh your brand and reignite your passion without losing what makes your business unique. We create websites that not only look stunning but also perform flawlessly, ensuring fast load times, seamless navigation, and easy-to-use layouts that attract and retain customers.
